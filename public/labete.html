LA BETE

FEMTOGO & VILHELM

Released on March 15, 2024, La Bête, produced by Vilhelm. and featuring the voices of FEMTOGO and baby hayabusa, sees FEMTOGO develop its chivalric novel. With epic themes and sharp phrasing, Vilhelm. and FEMTOGO transport us into their heroic fantasy world. The visuals by Kerningcastle, Holow, Yev., Caliyela and Lili Castiglioni, blending medieval inspiration and digital culture, allow us to fully immerse ourselves in this project of rare identity.

LA BETE: REWORKED

In September 2024, Vilhelm. released the reworked version of La Bête, in which he reproduced the 5 tracks without the vocals of FEMTOGO and baby hayabusa, and added an electronic touch to this medieval project. It allows us to rediscover the project from another, more melodic angle.

THE VISUALS

“I spearheaded the visual direction and graphic realization for Vilhelm & Femtogo's collaborative music project "LA BÊTE". Inspired by legendary tales, the visual direction revolves around the exploits of heroic figures such as Saint George and Sigurd as they battle a mythical beast.” says kerningcastle on its graphic identity research report.

The trailer announcing the project, entirely realized in 3D by the duo Holow, plunges us straight into the project's own heroic fantasy universe, with a very dark color palette reminiscent of FEMTOGO's dark lyrics.
